Cahoots Summertime, July 21st - September 21st 2021

After a whole challenging year in 2020, we at Cahoots Theatre were determined to bring back Theatre in any way we can in 2021. We have a slew of incredible events planned this summer that we're calling "Cahoots Summertime". If you haven't already, check out the video here or on the homepage.

All the events are held online live on Zoom or YouTube.

We would love to see you at any one of these events and support our artists and creatives in this incredibly challenging time. Theatre and the Arts are more important than ever in our communities, to foster creativity, spark discussions and provide opportunities. Let's keep Theatre alive!

Supermodel

August 19th, 7:00pm - 10:00pm EDT

It is an insidious brand of racism, the reverberations of which are resonant and complex. Supermodel, a rumination by East Asian-Canadian theatre artists, is a poignant & provocative interrogation of the myth of the model minority.
