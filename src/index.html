20/21 Season Announcement

Deaf Artists and Theatres Toolkit

Project Manager, Anita Small, Deaf Community Consultant, Catherine MacKinnon, and Joanne Cripps, External Deaf Consultant, along with the staff at Cahoots have created a comprehensive free online resource to advance the level of engagement and collaborations between theatre organizations and the Deaf community, including artists and audiences.

The online guide was informed by the production of ULTRASOUND co-produced by Cahoots and Theatre Passe Muraille, in spring 2016. ULTRASOUND was an ambitious production by deaf writer Adam Pottle, performed by two Deaf actors, featuring a Deaf Interpreter, dynamic titling, and the engagement of ASL Interpreters.

Support Us

Cahoots Theatre is a home for artists from the edge. We are a vibrant force for artistic expression and civic engagement within the Toronto community. The company has a 34 year history of developing and producing exciting new plays that investigate the complexities and intersections of Canada’s cultural diversity.

Cahoots Theatre relies on revenue from government grants, foundations and corporate sponsorships as well as ticket revenue to continue to create, develop and produce theatre that is of the highest calibre, with a theatrical vision that is powerful, innovative and globally relevant. Our revenue is also supplemented by individual donations from our patrons and supporters like you!

If you would like to make a one time donation or a monthly pledge to support the company and the work we do, we would greatly appreciate your contribution. In return, you will receive a tax receipt for the full amount of your donation.
